Incubation

The applications for the 12-month incubation program are opened twice a year. Startups from different tech genres are encouraged to apply. Once incubated, the NIC provides an enabling environment to the founders and help them transform their ideas into viable businesses.

The Jazz xlr8 Program

Jazz xlr8 is premium acceleration program run at the National Incubation Center. Jazz, being the leading tech company, uses its technical and marketing muscle to help startups scale their businesses. The program is tailored made for startups that are market ready.
